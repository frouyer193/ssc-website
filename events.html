Sydney SUP Club: Events & Training

Whether you’re training for your next national marathon or just looking to enjoy the sunrise on the water, the Sydney SUP Club has a place for you. We are a community of passionate paddlers dedicated to the sport, the water, and each other.

Weekly Training Sessions

For members looking to improve their fitness, stroke technique, and speed, we run dedicated training sessions throughout the week. These are perfect for staying consistent and pushing your limits with the support of the crew.

  • Tuesday Mornings: Intervals and technique focus.
  • Thursday Mornings: Distance and endurance building.
  • Saturday Mornings: Race simulation and buoy turns.

Note: Meet-up times and specific locations are shared via our WhatsApp group depending on conditions.

The Weekly Ritual: SUP Sundays at Lake Narrabeen

Every Sunday, we gather at the iconic Lake Narrabeen for our weekly SUP Sunday. It’s the heart of our club—a time to refine your technique, share tips on gear, or simply enjoy a social paddle in one of Sydney’s most beautiful coastal lagoons.

  • When: Every Sunday Morning
  • Where: Lake Narrabeen
  • Vibe: All levels welcome, from seasoned racers to social paddlers.

Competitive Edge: NSW Paddle Circuit

For those who feel the "need for speed," our club is highly active in the broader paddle community. We follow the full calendar of Paddle NSW events, providing a pathway for members who want to test their mettle in regional and state-level competitions.

You can find the full list of upcoming sanctioned races and events on the NSW Paddle Upcoming Events page.
