Sydney SUP Club (SSC)

JOIN OUR COMMUNITY ON THE WATER

The Sydney SUP Club (SSC) is a collective of 50 passionate paddlers of all ages and skill levels. We are united by a shared love for water sports and the belief that life is better when we meet on the water. Our club provides the perfect structure for anyone wanting to learn, practice, and excel at Stand-Up Paddling in a supportive and social environment.

What We Offer

Social Paddling Connection is at the heart of what we do. Join us for our regular "Sunday SUP" at Lake Narrabeen—a local favorite where the focus is on community, fresh air, and enjoying the glide.
Competitive Racing For those looking to push their limits, we offer dedicated race training. We help our members prepare for the challenge of official races organized by Paddle NSW, fostering a spirit of healthy competition and personal growth.
A Sport for Every Style Our members represent the full diversity of the sport. From dedicated surf SUP enthusiasts to high-endurance racers, you will find someone who shares your specific interests and can help you level up your skills.

Why SSC?

We believe SUP is more than just a workout; it’s a way to connect with nature and like-minded people. At SSC, you aren't just joining a club—you’re joining a community that supports your goals, whether they are competitive or recreational. From technical advice to post-paddle coffees, we make sure every member feels at home.
